🟣 Instagram
Timing
  • Reply within 30 mins of posting — Instagram boosts reach
  • Be first to comment on ideal clients' posts (set notifications)
  • Best DM window: 8–10am and 7–9pm weekdays
Comments
  • Always end with a genuine question — sparks conversation
  • 4+ words minimum — single word replies get hidden
  • Never copy-paste — Instagram detects it and suppresses
  • No "Great post!" or "Love this!" — zero value signals
  • Reference something specific in the post
DMs
  • Reference the exact post or story — shows genuine attention
  • One question per DM — don't overwhelm
  • Voice notes get 3× more replies than text
  • Wait for a reply before mentioning services
🔵 LinkedIn
Comments
  • Add your perspective, not just agreement
  • One thoughtful comment beats 10 generic ones
  • Tag relevant people only if genuinely relevant
  • Comment early (within 2hrs of post) for max visibility
Connection Requests
  • Always send a note — reference a shared interest
  • Connect after engaging 2+ times, not cold
  • Max 20 connections/day to avoid restriction
DMs
  • No pitch in the first message — ever
  • Reference their content or background specifically
  • Ask one open question about their business challenge
⚡ Golden Rules
  • Consistency beats volume — 20 real comments beat 100 fake ones
  • Warm leads are built over 3–7 touch-points
  • Never pitch cold — always qualify first
  • Track every warm lead in the escalation table
  • Sunday evening engagement = high Monday reach
  • Screenshot saves + story shares = buying intent
  • DM immediately after a call — "great chatting" message locks warmth
