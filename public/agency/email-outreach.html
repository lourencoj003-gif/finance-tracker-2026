Copywriting Tips
📏
Keep it short. Cold emails under 120 words get 3× more replies. Remove every sentence that isn't doing a job.
🎯
One CTA only. Ask for one thing — a 15-min call. Never give multiple options in a cold email.
🔍
Personalise the opener. Mention their Google reviews, a recent post, or their area. Generic = spam folder.
📬
Subject line test. It should read like it's from a human. Never write "Grow your business with AI" — write "Quick question, Sarah".
⏰
Send Tuesday–Thursday, 8–9am or 4–5pm local time. Avoid Mondays and Fridays.
