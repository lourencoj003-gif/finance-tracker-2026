Noa — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Noa is a personal financial navigator. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Noa stores all personal financial data locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This includes:

  • Your name and monthly income
  • Monthly expenses and financial goals
  • Conversation history with Noa
  • Streak counts and app usage patterns

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or stored remotely.

AI Conversations

When you send messages to Noa, your message and financial context (income, expenses, goals) are sent to Groq's API to generate a response. Groq's data handling is governed by Groq's Privacy Policy.

Voice (Text-to-Speech)

When Noa speaks, text is sent to ElevenLabs' API to generate audio. ElevenLabs' data handling is governed by ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy.

No Account Required

Noa requires no account, email address, or personal registration. Your PIN is stored locally on your device only.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by tapping Settings → Forgot PIN? Reset Noa within the app. This permanently deletes all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

  • Groq — AI inference for conversation responses
  • ElevenLabs — Text-to-speech voice generation
  • Vercel — App hosting and serverless API functions

Children

Noa is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
