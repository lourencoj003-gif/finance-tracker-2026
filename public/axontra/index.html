The Market Reality
"Underwriters are becoming data-driven. Brokers are still placing business the same way they did in 2015."

The market has shifted. Underwriters are deploying AI to assess submissions, model risk, and price with precision. The data they now expect from a broker is fundamentally different to what was acceptable five years ago.

Brokers that present clean, structured submissions with supporting data win terms faster and build deeper underwriter relationships. Those relying on relationship-led placement and manual CRM data are increasingly working at a disadvantage they haven't fully recognised yet.
