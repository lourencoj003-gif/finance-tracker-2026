Niche Outreach Playbook

One pain-driven message per niche. Adapt the variables in [brackets]. Send via LinkedIn DM, Instagram DM, or email. All messages lead toward a discovery call or the Proof Sprint.

Rules for every message

  • Lead with their problem, not our service. Never open with "we do AI marketing".
  • Reference something specific — their actual business, a post, a number of reviews, something real.
  • One CTA only. Either: "Worth a 20-min call?" or "I put together a Proof Sprint if you'd prefer to see the work before a call."
  • Keep messages under 120 words on LinkedIn/Instagram. Shorter on WhatsApp.
  • Never pitch the full retainer cold. Lead with the Sprint or a call — lower commitment = higher reply rate.
  • Follow up once at Day 3, once at Day 7. After that, move on. Don't chase.
